Garlic Butter Pasta

Description:

A quick and elegant weeknight dinner. The rich aroma of sautéed garlic and butter pairs perfectly with your favorite pasta shape.

  • 250g Pasta.
  • 4 cloves of garlic, finely minced.
  • 50g unsalted butter.
  • Fresh parsley, chopped.
  • Grated parmesan cheese.

Steps:

  1. Boil the pasta in salted water until al dente, then drain (reserve a bit of pasta water).
  2. In a pan, melt the butter over medium heat and saute the garlic until fragrant (do not burn).
  3. Toss the pasta into the pan, adding a splash of the reserved pasta water to emulsify the sauce.
  4. Top with fresh parsley and a generous dusting of Parmesan before serving.
