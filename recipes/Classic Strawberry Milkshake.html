Classic Strawberry Milkshake

Description

A refreshing and classic 3-ingredient treat. It's simple to make but always hits the spot on a warm day.

Ingredient

  • 2 cups of vanilla ice cream.
  • 1 cup of fresh strawberries (hulled)
  • 1/2 cup of milk.

Steps:

  1. Place the strawberries and milk in a blender and blend until smooth.
  2. Add the vanilla ice cream and blend again until the mixture is creamy.
  3. Pour into a tall glass and serve immediately.
