Japanese Onigiri

Desciription:

Iconic Japanese rice balls that are portable, filling, and can be customized with various savory fillings.

  • 2 cups of cooked Japanese short-grain rice (warm).
  • 1 sheet of Nori (dried seaweed), cut into strips.
  • Filling: Tuna mayo or grilled salmon flakes.
  • Salt for seasoning.

Steps:

  1. Wet your hands with a bit of water and springkle them with salt to prevent the rice from sticking.
  2. Take a handful of warm rice, create an indentation in the center, and place your filling inside.
  3. Gentle press the rice around the filling and mold it into a triangular shape.
  4. Wrap a strip of nori around the bottom of the rice ball as a handle.
